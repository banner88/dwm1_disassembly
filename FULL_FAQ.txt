 ++-----------------------------------------------------------------++
 ||                          Introduction                           ||
 ++-----------------------------------------------------------------++
 This Walkthrough is for Dragon Warrior Monsters! Let's begin. Nothing
 much to write here really.
 
 This game is meant to be played after playing Dragon Quest I-VI so
 you can get the references.
 
 I've already played Dragon Quest I-VI and I also wrote Walkthroughs
 for them which are available on GameFaq.com:
 
 - Dragon Quest I, II and III (SNES)
 - Dragon Quest IV, V and VI (DS)
 
 When the game begins, you'll see a shooting star before you'll be 
 taken to the Title Screen so you'll confirm that you have the right
 game. Press any button to be taken to the Menu Screen.
 
 Select New Game so the game can begin.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ++-----------------------------------------------------------------++
 ||                           Prologue                              ||
 ++-----------------------------------------------------------------++
 (-------------------------------------------------------------------)
  )                             Home                                (
 (-------------------------------------------------------------------)
 A boy and girl are running in circles. Eventually, the little girl
 Milayou will tell the little boy, Terry that it's time for bed.
 Milayou will ask Terry if he forgot what their parents told them.
 Their parents told them that if they stay up too late, monsters will
 carry them away. Terry wants to meet the monsters. Milayou will say
 he's being silly and then they'll both go to bed.
 
 Wait a while then press the D-Pad Right Button to get out of bed. We
 gain control over Terry. Speak to Milayou to find out she's asleep.
 Examine the stuffed animal to find out it belongs to Milayou. Examine
 the bookshelves to find a Fairy Tale and Milayou's Diary. The left
 dresser contains Terry's clothes and the right one contains Milayou's
 clothes. Go through the east opening to get to next room.
 
 In this room, a Black Monster will come out from the dresser. It will
 ask Terry if he's Milayou. The monster will say he doesn't look like
 her. The monster knows Milayou is a girl. Terry will tell the
 monster his name and the monster will say Terry is not the one he's
 after. The monster will introduce itself. Its name is Warubou. He's
 from the Kingdom of GreatLog. He tells Terry not to forget it. He'll
 push Terry out of the way then he'll go into the room Milayou is in.
 After some time, Warubou will drag Milayou out of the west room and
 then he'll take her into the Dresser with him. A White Monster will
 jump out of the dresser. The White Monster will ask itself where
 Milayou is. It will notice that Terry can speak monster talk. He'll
 ask Terry where Milayou is. Terry will tell the monster that she
 was taken away. The White Monster will say he was too late. He'll
 introduce himself. His name is Watabou. He wants Terry to follow
 him so Terry can find Milayou. Watabou hopes Terry is a skilled
 master. Terry doesn't understand what he's talking about but
 Watabou says he will if he follows him. Watabou will go back
 into the dresser and you will gain control over Terry.
 
 Examine the bookshelves to find an Encyclopedia of Monsters. It's
 author is unknown. The other bookshelf contains books that are
 too difficult to read. Examine the clock to find out it's midnight.
 The stuffed animal in this room belongs to Milayou. Examine the
 dresser. Terry will go into it.

  +-----------------------------------------------------------------+
                           (-------------)
                            )  Village  (
                           (-------------)
 Where are we? It looks like we got teleported here. After Terry stops
 spinning, a person will come over to speak to him. The person says
 Terry must be the master. He also said we must have had a long trip
 here. Terry doesn't know where he is. The person will tell us that
 we're in the Kingdom of GreatTree. Watabou brought us here. The person
 will say they need to go to the castle and pay their respects to the
 King. 
 
 The Old Man will lead us outside. Outside, he will explain to us that
 this kingdom was created inside a big tree. We are currently at the
 bottom and the castle is at the top. The Old Man will lead us to the
 castle. He'll go up series of ladders. On our way, a Warrior will
 ask the Old Man if Terry is the new Master Watabou brought here.
 The Old Man will answer him with a Yes and says he's taking Terry to
 see the King. The Warrior will wish us goodluck at the Starry Night
 Tournament. Whatever that is.
 
 At the castle entrance, the Old Man will tell Terry that it is time to
 see the King. Terry will go into the opening.

                          (--------------)
                           )   Castle   (
                          (--------------)
 Inside, the Minister of this kingdom will speak to us. He'll ask 
 Terry if he's the new Master. The King has a favor to ask us.
 The Minister wants us to comply with the King's request. After
 the Minister goes up north you'll gain control.
 
 Speak to the guards to find out we are in the castle of GreatTree and
 that the King needs our help. Go 1 screen south. The man will say this
 is the Kingdom of GreatTree. The Old Man is blocking the ladder
 going down. He tells us to go and see the King. Go back into the Castle
 and go one screen north. 
 
 The King will welcome us. The King wants us to identify ourself. You
 get to change the name of your character. I'm sticking with the 
 default name, Terry. The King will tell Terry his wish after you've
 named him. He wants us to participate and win the Starry Night 
 Tournament. Terry will tell him that he'd rather find Milayou. The
 King thought we came here to comply with his request. He'll say
 there's an easier way than searching for her. He wants us to win
 the tournament. Legend has it that the one who victorious in the
 tournament of the Starry Night will be granted a wish. Terry wish
 to find his sister will be granted if we win. The King also tells
 Terry to search for his sister while he prepares for the tournament
 The King will give us some monsters. Without any monsters, Terry 
 can't serve as a Master. The King will tell us that the Monster
 Farm is at the upper level. He'll tell us to ask someone named
 Pulio for our monsters.
 
 We gain control. Speak to the Minister to find out this kingdom has
 been losing in the Starry Night Tournament. They are hosting it
 this time and he wants the Kingdom to win. Speak to the left jester
 to find out the tournament is held on a starry night. The Masters
 who are chosen to represent their kingdom fight to be the greatest
 monster master. The right jester will tell us the legend of the
 Starry Night Tournament. The Tournament comes after every 47th
 full moon. Hundreds of thousands of stars fall and fill the sky.
 Fragments of the light cover the world. That's the Starry Night.
 The night when countless lives shower down. To celebrate the night,
 the tournament is held. Go one screen west.
 
 There are 2 Jesters in this room. Speak to the one near the body
 of water to find out everyone will be happy if we won the
 Starry Night Tournament. The one near the chests has a feeling
 our victory will help us find our sister. Both chests are locked.
 The left bookshelf contains a book titled: The Kingdom of GreatTree.
 Agree to read it. The Kingdom is a little kingdom built inside
 a big tree. Other kingdoms are also built in big trees. All the
 kingdoms have something called Travelers' Gate. Beyond the gates
 are the mystic worlds where the monsters live. When the Starry
 Night Tournament nears, GreatTree trembles. It's a sign that
 GreatTree is growing. The right bookshelf contains a book titled:
 The Master of Monsters. Agree to read it. People who understand
 monster talk and are able to tame them are Monster Masters.
 There are few master's who can understand and tame monsters.
 It is important not only to understand them but also to be like
 by them. There are masters from this world and masters who are
 brought by Watabou who is the spirit of the GreatTree. That was
 some information. Go back east 1 screen to the Throne Room.
 
 Go south 1 screen.
 
 Go up the stairs here.
 
 There's a little boy and 2 monsters. One of the monster is a 
 Dracky and the other is a Slime. Speak to the Dracky to find
 out we are at the Monster Farm. Don't speak to the boy or Slime
 yet. Go west 1 screen. Speak to the monster to find out Pulio 
 let the monsters escape. The jar is filled with garbage. Speak
 to the man to find out he wonders what he can catch here. He
 will put his catch into the jar. He wants us to come and see
 his catch sometimes. Go north 1 screen then go east 1 screen.
 Speak to the monster to find out he is wondering where he can
 get treats. Read the sign. It says Danger, don't rush. Go for
 it with courage. Try to fall down the cliff next to the sign
 and the monster will stop us. Go east 1 screen. Speak to the
 monster near the sign to find out it can't reach Lipsy because
 of the hole. Read the sign to find out when the GreatTree grows,
 the ground trembles. This hole is the evidence of the GreatTree's
 growth. The previous tremors made this hole. Go south 1 screen
 then west 1 screen to be back at the entrance of the Monster Farm.
 Speak to the Slime. His name is Slio. He's the grandson of
 Slib. Slio will ask us if we want to know about the farm. Answer
 with a YES. We can drop up to 19 monsters and eggs here. The 
 monsters can still get the experience points we earn even if we
 leave the monsters here. But the monsters will get more experience
 points if we bring them with us. When we reach the limit, we can
 make some of them sleep. Sleep causes all the monsters in the farm
 to sleep. We can then drop off 19 more monsters at the farm. And
 if we reach the limit again, we will have to part with some 
 monsters. That's sad. We can't take the eggs with us. He wants us
 to drop off all the eggs we find here. Speak to the little boy
 and he'll ask you if you came to steal all his monsters. Answer
 with a NO. The little boy is Pulio. He takes care of the farm.
 He heard about us from the King. He will give us our monsters.
 ...wait...he actually has only 1 monster to give. He let them all
 get away. We know that. Slib is the monster he has left. He was
 the last King's favorite. He wants us to choose [PICK UP] to take
 the monster.
 
 A screen will pop up:
 
 DROP OFF
 PICK UP
 CHECK
 SEPARATE
 SLEEP
 EXIT
 
 Select [PICK UP] then select Slib. Slib is at Level 1 and he's a
 Slime. He's also a grandpa. Err.... When you select him, select
 INFO to see his stats. After that select OK! After we've picked
 Slib up, select Exit.
 
 You can see Slib HP and MP at the bottom of the screen. That's nice.
 Press the A button to open up the Menu Screen. 
 
 INFO
 ITEM
 SKIL
 OPTN
 
 Select OPTN then select JOURNAL. SAVE YOUR GAME!!!!!
 
 That's all. Go back down the stairs.
 
 Go 1 screen north so we can meet the King again. The King isn't happy
 that we adopted Slib. Terry will tell him that Pulio only had 1 monster.
 The King will get mad at Pulio. He let them all escape. He'll tell
 one of the Jester to bring Pulio here. The Jester will obey. When
 Pulio arrive, he'll ask the King for forgiveness. The King will ask
 Pulio if Hale escaped as well. Pulio will answer with a YES. It seems
 Hale belongs to the King. The King will instruct his guards to throw
 Pulio into the dungeons. Press any button to make Terry speak with
 the King. Terry wants to catch Hale to free Pulio. The King allows us
 to try. Pulio will say Hale escaped through the Travelers' Gate.
 The King wants us to proceed to the Travelers' Gate. If we bring
 Hale back, Pulio will be set free. The treasure chests in the west
 room is now ours. Yay! He tells us to ask the Minister about it. We're
 dismissed. The King will go through one of the curtains. Speak to
 Pulio and he'll teach you how to record your progress. I've already
 taught you. Speak to the Minister. He'll say the King is very upset.
 He wants us to bring Hale back. Go 1 screen west.
 
 Open the left chest to find a [HERB]. Open the right chest to find
 another [HERB]. The Herbs are more your monsters not Terry. Go 1 screen
 west.
 
 Go 1 screen south.
 
 Go down the stairs that are now accessible.
 
 Speak to the guards here. One of them will welcome us to the Chambers
 of the Travelers' Gate. Another one will say we will say we will find
 items scattered around in the Travelers' Gate. He wants us to use them
 well. We can only carry up to 20 of them. The last guard will say
 Hale is the cherished pet of the King. The room over there with the 
 door open is the Chamber of Travelers' Gates. Go 1 screen north.
 
 Speak to the Guard to find out Hale escaped through the Travelers' Gate
 ahead. The Spiral is the Travelers' Gate. Step on it!

 Unfortunately for us, the floors are random and the item spawns on
 the ground are random as well. It's a Maze. That's what I'm trying

 Walk over items to get them. There are Random Monster Encounters and
 if you defeat them, you'll gain Experience Points. We have 2 New

 We have 4 Floors to deal with before we get to the 5th Floor which
 is where we will find Hale. Alright. Good luck! Before getting to
 the last floor:
 
 - Get Slib to Level 5
 - Recruit a Dracky and an AntEater. 
 
 You normally need to feed Monsters Meat in order for them to join your
 party but they might ask to join after you defeat them.
 
 Make sure you get all the items you find on the floor and use them
 if you need them. 
 
 Alright:

 A Healer can be found on this last level. The Healer is Hale.
 
 Heal up then speak to Hale. Hale will introduce himself. He knows
 we're here to catch him. Hale will tell us why he escaped. The farm
 is boring and he hates it there. He doesn't want to go back!
 
 --------------------
 | Boss Battle      |
 --------------------
 Name: Hale (Healer)
 
 Recruitable: Yes
 --------------------
 
 If Slib is at Level 5 accompanied with Dracky and AntEater, this
 battle will be extremely easy. After the battle, Hale will want to
 join our party. Accept him. I repeat, accept him. He's a very
 useful monster and a great addition to the team. You get name him.
 Use his default name, Hale or change it if you want. I'm calling
 him Hale.
 
 We can only have 3 Monsters in our party? Oh...bummer....send
 Dracky or AntEater back to the Farm.
 
 Hale says Terry is strong. He likes us. Watabou will arrive. He'll
 praise Terry. He'll take us back to the castle.

 us. He's glad he can return to his Monster Farm. The King wants us
 to go to the Arena. Our rivals are waiting for us at the Arena. We
 can compete in the tournament if we defeat all of them. We're dismissed.
 The King will go into the curtain. That's probably his Chambers.
 
 The Minister will heal our Monsters. He tells us how to get to the
 arena. We need to go straight out of the castle then go down a
 ladder. If we survive in the arena, we can join the tournament.
 
 Return to the Monster Farm first. Speak to Pulio and he'll tell us
 a secret. A SkyDragon is flying over the farm. If we wait under it,
 something good will happen. That's nice to hear. Alright! Drop off
 the other monster you captured so that it can just be Slib and 
 Hale in your party. You dont need to do this. That is my monster
 set up. The monsters in my party now are only Slib and Hale. You
 can put in any monster you want but I'll be telling you my monster
 party everytime in case you want to follow mine.
 
 Speak to the Dracky to find out we can find something special 
 under the SkyDragon. Go west 1 screen. Speak to the monster to find
 out Pulio was happy. Search the jar to find the [TINY MEDAL #1].
 We can find them inside Travelers' Gate as well but the ones you
 find in fixed places are the ones I will number. Go north 1 screen
 to find a Monster and the SkyDragon flying. Stay under the 
 SkyDragon and it will lay an egg. The egg will hit Terry's head
 but we got it. [EGG] of a SkyDragon. Eggs can't be carried 
 around so we left it with Pulio. If you go under the SkyDragon 
 after collecting the Egg, it will poop on you. Don't be greedy
 and expect 2 Eggs. :) Speak to the Monster and he will ask us
 if we have an Egg. Answer with a YES and he will tell us that
 Eggs will be egg forever if left alone. There's a place to hatch
 eggs in this kingdom. Until we find that place, we should keep
 all the eggs we find at the farm. Go east 1 screen. Speak to the
 2 Monsters. One of them will say if we take monsters on a journey,
 they will become attached to us. When they are left on the
 farm all the time, they will not listen to us. But if we give them
 treats, they will become really attached to us. The other Monster
 will tell us the different treats. BeefJerky is the least tasty,
 Ribs are better than PorkChops. Sirloin is the best. He still
 won't let us fall down the cliff. Go east 1 screen then south 1
 screen. The monster here is old and can no longer gain levels. He's
 at his level limit. Go west 1 screen to be back with Pulio.
 
 Let's talk about Hale a bit. He's at Level 6 and he has 1 spell
 which is the Heal Spell.
 
 Go back down the stairs.
 
 Go south 1 screen to leave the Castle.
 
                           (-------------)
                            )  Village  (
                           (-------------)
 The man will say something looks dangerous before falling down the
 cliff. Woah...that looks fun. Jump down the cliff or use the
 stairs to go 1 screen south. Jumping down is more fun.
 
 Speak to the man to find out he is glad that he didn't get hurt.
 Speak to the Old Man to find out he is getting old and he's
 tired of going down the stairs so he's resting. We can't go
 down the stairs since he's blocking us. Speak to the lady to
 find out we're in the Village of GreatTree. Don't go into
 the hole yet. It leads to the Arena. Go east 1 screen. 
 
 Speak to the Warrior and he'll ask you if you have a TinyMedal.
 Answer with a YES to find out we'll be welcomed in the room
 1 screen north if we have TinyMedals. Speak to the Bard
 to find out he once picked up a TinyMedal back when he was
 on a journey in the Travelers' Gate. He'll tell us something
 useful. Beyond the Travelers' Gate, sometimes we can find
 small rooms. We can record our quest while in those rooms.
 Those are the only places we can record our save states when
 we're beyond the Travelers' Gate. Go north 1 screen.
 
 Speak to the lady to find out we can see the ground far below.
 I don't think it's safe to stay at the edges....go into the
 hole.
 
 Speak to the man here to find out he's in the Medal Man. He
 collects TinyMedals scattered all around the world. If we
 give him some medals, we'll reward us. Make sure you look
 for TinyMedals beyond Travelers' Gates and the ones that are
 at fixed positions which I will mention. The Medal Man will
 collect the TinyMedals we have right now. If we have collect
 13 in total, he'll give us 1 ZapBird Egg.
 
 -------------------------------------------

  18 Medals: Trumpeter Egg
  25 Medals: Spikerous
  30 Medals: Metabble
 -------------------------------------------

 Speak to the Slime to find out he loves TinyMedals as well.
 If we collect lots of them the Slime will give us a reward 
 as well. Return to the Arena Entrance.
 
 Go into the Arena.
 
                          (-------------)
                           )   Arena   (
                          (-------------)
 Speak to the girl behind the right counter. She'll welcome us
 to the Arena and ask us if we want to hear about the battle.
 Answer with a YES. The battle classes go from S, A down to
 G for beginners. We'll fight 3 masters per class. We can go
 to the next class once we've defeated the 3rd master. If we
 survive the S class we'll be eligible to participate in the
 Starry Night Tournament. During the battles, we cannot give
 commands to the monsters. We can't use any items either. 
 If we choose to RUN then we've given up the battle. To
 register, we just have to speak to the other girl behind the
 left counter. 
 
 Don't register yet. Examine the opening north to find out
 only those who are registered can go through. Don't register
 yet. Go east 1 screen. 
 
 Speak to the Warrior here to find out his name is Teto. He's
 nervous because the battle starts soon. He'll tell us that we can
 learn useful information from the people out back. Go 1 
 screen north.
 
 Speak to the Warrior to find out they all gather here seeking
 victory. The battle is not to be taken lightly. Many lack the
 courage to see it through to the end. Search the 4 Barrels you
 can here. From bottom to top. There's a monster sleeping inside.
 There's nothing in it. There's the [TINY MEDAL #2] in it! There's
 something engraved on it: Warubou was here. The man with his
 monster is teaching the monster how to say onigiri. The monster
 doesn't know what onigiri means. He wonders why his master won't
 say rice balls. Go north 1 screen.
 
 Speak to the lady to find out the restaurant is out back. Speak
 to the Viking to find out the guy at the entrance is his rival.
 Speak to the Warrior to find out his rival is the Viking nearby.
 The Warrior will tell us something useful. He tells us to watch
 out for Foreign Masters behind Travelers' Gates. They will
 mincemeat out of us. Search the left jar to find an old woman's
 notebook: Educational Guide. Agree to read it: If you choose
 CHARGE you can train the monsters to be brave. Brave monsters
 have a better chance to make clean hits. If you choose MIXED,
 the monsters will become wiser. Wise monsters easily decide
 which support skill is best to use. When you choose CAUTIOUS,
 the monsters become cautious. Cautious monsters do not use
 MP but sometimes heal their damages. That's some nice info.
 Search the right jar to find a piece of paper. Something
 is written on it: Good things will happen if you Goopi five
 times in a row. That's nice to know. Go into the restaurant
 by going 1 screen north. 
 
 The Warrior is wondering who is the Old Woman at the reception
 wearing a flashy costume. The Bard wants to meet the Queen of
 the Kingdom. He says something else: Army, Bomb, Army, Bubble,
 Army. The Bartender says Superior masters have a
 well-balanced combination of monsters. A monster that restores
 and a monster that attacks. That's why I have Slib and Hale.
 Exit the Restaurant.
 
 Go 1 screen west.
 
 Speak to the Old Man to find out the statues here are the 
 protectors of the GreatTree. Before we go on a quest,
 we are advised to make an entry in the Journal. Speak to
 the other old man and he'll ask us if we want to know
 where the Queen is. Answer with a YES to find out we
